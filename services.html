Finish and system choices

Select the finish that matches how you want the space to look day-to-day. We'll help match the right system to your performance goals.

Flake

Most popular for garages and many outdoor spaces. Adds visual depth and helps hide normal wear between cleanings.

Solid Color

Clean, consistent appearance ideal for minimalist styles and spaces where you want a straightforward finish.

Metallic

Decorative, high-impact look with unique movement. Best when appearance is the top priority for a statement area.
