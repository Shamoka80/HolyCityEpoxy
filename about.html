Why proper preparation matters

The success of any coating starts before the top layer goes down. Proper surface preparation is what supports bond strength, finish consistency, and durability over time in Charleston's heat, humidity, and changing weather.

  • Concrete evaluation

    We assess slab condition, moisture risk, and existing coatings so the selected system matches the jobsite reality.

  • Mechanical surface prep

    We mechanically profile the concrete for adhesion and remove weak material that can cause premature failure.

  • Repair and stabilization

    We repair cracks and surface defects before installation to support a cleaner finish and more dependable performance.
