About Holy City Epoxy

A local coating company focused on quality prep, durable systems, and homeowner trust.

We serve Charleston-area homeowners who want long-lasting results, clear communication, and a crew that respects their property from start to finish.

What matters most on every project.

We prioritize the steps that protect your investment and deliver a polished finished look.

Professional Preparation

Surface prep is never rushed. We clean, profile, and repair concrete as needed so each coating system bonds correctly and performs over time.

System Quality

We use proven residential coating systems selected for Lowcountry humidity, daily use, and strong visual appeal in garages, porches, and pool areas.

Reliable Communication

From estimate to final walkthrough, we keep expectations clear, timelines realistic, and homeowners informed at each stage.

Built for Charleston homes and local conditions.

Coastal weather, heat, and humidity can be tough on concrete. Our process is designed for the way local surfaces age and how homeowners use their spaces.

  • Residential-first focus with mid-market, premium-value positioning.
  • Detail-oriented craftsmanship without unnecessary upsells.
  • Respectful jobsite habits and clean handoff at project completion.

Our Promise

We treat your project like it is our own home: proper prep, honest recommendations, and a finished surface you can feel confident showing off.
